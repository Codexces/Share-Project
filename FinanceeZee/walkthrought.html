Walk Through

MDPs Conducted as Program Director

  • Senior Professor of Accounting and Finance, and Corporate Governance at Management Development Institute (MDI) Gurgaon. INDIA

  • Independent Director (Non-Executive) at Machino Plastics Ltd., Gurugram, a Suzuki JV.

  • Forensic Accounting, Fraud Risk Management, and Corporate Governance Professional

  • Double Doctorate, one in Finance; and another in Accounting from Faculty of Management Studies (FMS), University of Delhi Bachelor’s with Hons. in Commerce from Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), University of Delhi Master’s in Commerce with specialization in Finance from Department of Commerce, Delhi School of Economics (DSE), University of Delhi.

  • Visiting Professor to Faculty of Management, University of Lodz, Poland and leading Management Institutes and Universities nationally, including Indian Institute of Managements (IIMs).

  • Management Trainer and Consultant to Organizations like Bata, Encore, Federal-Mogul Goetze (India) Ltd., SOS Village International, Cairn India, National Banking Institute (Nepal), Druk Holdings (Bhutan), UltraTech Cements, ONGC, IOCL, Jindal, LIC, BEL, IGL, TIL, Ester Industries, and Armed Forces. Conducted and delivered over 500 MDPs/Training Programs at senior levels for the organizations and trained more than 10,000 executives over the years. These mainly include Finance for Non-Finance Executives, Forensic Analytics, Fraud Risk Management, Independent Director’s and Corporate Governance and Enhancing Financial Skills Using Excel

  • Executed Consultancy Assignments, the major ones include Assessment of Employees for Promotion Exercise for IFCI Ltd., Evaluation of Bid for Municipal Corporation Gurugram, Corporate Governance for IOCL, Feasibility Study for Increasing Coin Production at SPMCIL, Financial Management of Bata, Accounting Application for SAP Professionals of Ester Industries, and Financial Leadership of TIL.

  • Prolific Writer; Authored 12 books, including highly acclaimed books Finance for Non-Finance People and Corporate Governance: Theory and Practice. Published more than 75 research articles in national and international journals of repute with cases published by Ivey Publishing; cross-listed in Harvard Business Review, Asia Case Research Centre, and Emerald Emerging Markets Case Studies.

  • Three decades of industry and academic experience in key positions in organizations/institutions; including Shri Ram Group, University of Delhi and MDI Gurgaon

  • Extensive international experience in different parts of the world; including the US, Europe, Canada and Asia. The cross-national research and training experience includes Duke University, US; Sapienza University of Rome, Italy; Frankfurt School of Finance and Management, Germany; and Ontario College for Research and Development, University of Toronto, Canada.

  • Offers a unique and popular course, ‘Forensic Accounting and Corporate Governance’ to second year students of MBA at MDI and abroad.

  • Associate Editor, Journal of Forensic Accounting Research, American Accounting Association, USA. https://aaahq.org/Journal-of-Forensic-Accounting-Research

  • Conferred with Award for Excellence in Research, 2015–16 and Award for Excellence in Teaching, 2015–16 at MDI
