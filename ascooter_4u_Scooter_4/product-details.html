Overview

If you are looking for the best of both worlds look no further. The Go Go Sport combines travel scooter's take-apart convenience with the long frame and roomy feel that so many riders seek. Super features include powerful 18 AH batteries for a 14.5-15.9 mile max range and a 325 lb weight capacity. Easy Feather-Touch disassembly lets you transport your scooter in 5 easy pieces- the heaviest piece weighs only 35 lbs and fits in the back of most cars.

What makes this Different

With the longest base of Pride’s travel scooters, the Go Go Sport is sturdy, yet nimble. The overall length of the Go Go Sport 3-wheel is 42”, but the turning radius is only 36” - perfect for indoors or out! The overall length of the Go Go Sport 4-wheel is 43”, and the turning radius is 50.5”. The Go Go Sport features an additional charging port conveniently located up on the tiller for easy access and convenience. Large 18AH batteries are standard, allowing you to travel up to 15.9 miles per charge. The flat-free tires are 3” wide for increased traction and safety.

Why We Like It

The Sporty styling and durability make the Go Go Sport a favorite. The 18AH battery gives you a longer travel range than most portable scooters without an additional upgrade charge. The bright LED headlamp lighting your path after dark lets you see and be seen for added safety.

What you need to know when ordering

The Go Go Sport will arrive disassembled in one large box. Portable scooters are meant to go together easily for transport. No tools are required and the 5 pieces click together. If you think you may need assistance with assembly, you may choose our White Glove Delivery to have it brought to your home and assembled.

You have the option to purchase a 2-3 Year In-Home Service. If you choose purchase a service plan and experience a problem with your scooter, simply call SpinLife to arrange for a technician to come to your home for diagnosis and repair.

*Pride FDA Class II Medical Devices are designed to aid individuals with mobility impairments.
